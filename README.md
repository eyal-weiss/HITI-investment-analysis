# High Tide (NASDAQ: HITI) — Investment Analysis

Independent investment analysis of High Tide Inc., Canada's largest non-franchised cannabis retailer operating 218 Canna Cabana stores with a disruptive Costco-like discount club model (2.5M members), supplemented by global e-commerce and a medical cannabis distribution business in Germany.

## Thesis Summary

**Date:** March 2026 | **Data basis:** FY2025 audited financials (year ended October 31, 2025)

High Tide has grown revenue from C$181M (FY2021) to C$594M (FY2025) while delivering 22 consecutive quarters of positive Adjusted EBITDA. The September 2025 Remexian acquisition adds ~C$105M in annualized European distribution revenue. The stock trades at US$2.48 on NASDAQ (~C$3.37), implying 0.55x EV/Revenue and 8.5x EV/Adj. EBITDA.

### Key Investment Pillars

1. **Store-count growth** in a fragmented Canadian market where Canna Cabana holds only 12% share
2. **Cabana Club discount model** driving industry-leading same-store sales growth and customer loyalty
3. **Remexian acquisition** transforms HITI into a leading European medical cannabis distributor, with Canadian export pipeline as unmodeled upside

## Methodology

- 5-year DCF with Gordon Growth terminal value (WACC 12.0%, terminal growth 2.5%)
- Bottom-up sub-segment margin model with explicit white-label, Cabanalytics/ads, and ELITE membership projections
- Cross-checked with EV/EBITDA and P/E exit multiple valuations
- Scenario analysis across bull, base, and bear cases
